Why “AI for South Africa’s Youth”?

South Africa's youth holds the promise of tomorrow. We are dedicated to empowering South Africa's future innovators with the knowledge and skills to comprehend AI, and to appreciate its significance in our digital era.

“As more and more artificial intelligence is entering into the world, more and more emotional intelligence must enter into leadership.”

Amit Ray, Famous AI Scientist, Author of Compassionate Artificial Intelligence

VISION STATEMENT

Our vision is to empower every young mind in South Africa, transforming them into innovators of tomorrow by democratizing access to Artificial Intelligence education. We aim to create an inclusive and stimulating learning environment that nurtures curiosity, fosters technical and social skills, and encourages responsible application of AI.

MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to democratize AI for all South African youth, regardless of gender, ethnicity, or location. We are dedicated to empowering South Africa's future innovators with the knowledge and skills to comprehend AI, and to appreciate its significance in our digital era.

  • Deep Understanding of AI: Our mission is to demystify AI for South Africa's youth, equipping them with the necessary skill sets and mindsets for AI readiness.
  • Access to AI Tools: We aim to democratise access to AI tools, powered by Intel technologies. We are committed to training South Africa's youth to use these tools proficiently, enabling them to engage with AI skilfully and confidently.
  • Creating AI Solutions: Our vision extends beyond theoretical learning; we value practical application. We encourage the creation of AI-powered social solutions that make a difference, demonstrating real progress and achievement.
